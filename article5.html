Tissot PRX : The most popular watch of 2022 - is it worth your time and other best alternatives

Introduction

Watches have always been a fashion accessory that has been with us for centuries. From the first pocket watches to the modern-day smartwatches, the watch industry has seen a lot of changes. However, some watch designs have remained popular throughout the years, and the Tissot PRX is one of them. In this article, we will take a closer look at the Tissot PRX, including its 35mm version, compare it to the Tissot PRX Powermatic 80, and name and describe two of the best alternatives to the PRX if it just isn’t your cup of tea.


The Tissot PRX

The Tissot PRX is a watch that was first introduced in 1978. It quickly became a popular watch design of the 1970s and 1980s, characterized by its integrated bracelet and octagonal case shape. The watch's design was a combination of sporty and elegant, making it perfect for various occasions. The Tissot PRX features a quartz ETA movement, a stainless-steel case, and a sapphire crystal. The watch comes with a steel bracelet or a leather strap, depending on the model.

One of the most popular features of the Tissot PRX is its 35mm version. The 35mm version of the Tissot PRX is a smaller version of the original watch, perfect for those who prefer a smaller watch on their wrist. The 35mm version features the same design as the original Tissot PRX, with an integrated bracelet and octagonal case shape.

Both watches feature stunning sunburst dials but because of their integrated bracelet, you won’t be able to do any switching with your own straps.


Tissot PRX Powermatic 80

The Tissot Powermatic 80 is another popular watch from Tissot and is almost exactly the same as the quartz PRX except for its slightly bigger thickness (10.4 to 10.9), comes in a waffle dial and is only available in 40mm diameter. It features an automatic movement with an 80-hour power reserve, a stainless-steel case, and a sapphire crystal. The Tissot PRX Powermatic 80 comes with a steel bracelet or a leather strap, depending on the model.

Best Alternatives to the Tissot PRX

If you're looking for an alternative to the Tissot PRX, there are several options available in the market. Here are two of the best alternatives to the Tissot PRX:


Tissot Gentleman and PR100

If you just aren’t a fan of the integrated style looks but still want a high-grade Swiss movement in your wristwatch, whether it be a quartz or automatic, then Tissot still has you covered thanks to its similar looking Tissot gentleman as well as its lesser known Tissot PR100.


Both watches cost more or less the same as the PRX and Powermatic 80 and use exactly the same movements as their PRX counterparts. The gentleman has more dial options as well as leather strap alternatives, the quartz version only has the brand name and its date of creation written on the dial at the twelve whilst the automatic version also has ‘Powermatic 80 silicium’ written at the six, these to watches have the date window placed at the three if you’re willing to spend a little more you can also find an open heart version of this watch for about $950. On the other hand, The PR100 has a slightly smaller case, PR100 for the quartz and ‘PR100 Powermatic 80’ written on the dial, a date window at the six and a T shaped counterweight for the second’s hand.

Timex Q reissue

If you're looking for a watch with a similar design to the Tissot PRX but at a more affordable price point, the Timex Q might be a good option to consider as well as the Timex M79 automatic.


The Timex Q is a reissue of a popular watch from the 1970s, and it features a similar integrated bracelet and square case shape as the Tissot PRX. It also has a quartz movement, a date window, and a water resistance rating of up to 50 meters.. One of the best things about the Timex Q is its affordability. It is priced significantly lower than the Tissot PRX, making it a great option for those on a budget who still want a watch with a similar design.. Of course, the Timex Q may not have the same level of craftsmanship or materials as the Tissot PRX, but it still offers a great value for its price point. If you're looking for a stylish and affordable watch with a similar design to the Tissot PRX, the Timex Q is definitely worth considering.

Conclusion

The Tissot PRX is a watch that has been popular for decades. Its sporty and elegant design makes it perfect for various occasions. The 35mm version of the Tissot PRX is perfect for those who prefer a smaller watch on their wrist. If you're looking for an alternative to the Tissot PRX, the Tissot gentleman, Tissot PR100 and Timex Q reissue are all great options to consider.
