A Brief History of Orient Watch Company

Established in 1950, the Orient Watch Company has a long and storied history in the world of horology. As one of Japan's largest watch manufacturers, Orient has built a reputation for producing high-quality, reliable, and stylish timepieces at affordable prices. The company's commitment to in-house manufacturing and craftsmanship has earned it a loyal following among watch enthusiasts worldwide.


The Kamasu Series: A Modern Dive Watch Classic

The Orient Kamasu series is a collection of dive watches that combine the best of traditional watchmaking with modern design and technology. The name "Kamasu" is derived from the Japanese word for "barracuda," a fitting moniker for a watch that is both sleek and powerful. The Kamasu RA-AA0003R19B, in particular, has garnered significant attention for its striking design, robust build, and exceptional value.


Design and Aesthetics

The Orient Kamasu RA-AA0003R19B features a 41.8mm stainless steel case, which is both durable and comfortable on the wrist. The case is topped with a scratch-resistant sapphire crystal, ensuring that the watch remains pristine even in the most demanding conditions. The unidirectional rotating bezel, a hallmark of dive watches, is adorned with a red insert that adds a touch of color and flair to the overall design.




The dial of the Kamasu RA-AA0003R19B is a deep, sunburst red that captures the eye and adds a sense of depth to the watch. The applied hour markers and hands are generously filled with lume, ensuring excellent legibility in low-light conditions. The date and day complication at 3 o'clock is a practical addition that enhances the watch's functionality.


The stainless steel bracelet is solid and well-constructed, with a secure fold-over clasp and safety catch. The end links are solid, adding to the overall feeling of quality and durability. The bracelet can be easily adjusted to fit a variety of wrist sizes, ensuring a comfortable and secure fit.


Movement and Performance

At the heart of the Orient Kamasu RA-AA0003R19B lies the in-house Caliber F6922 automatic movement. This 22-jewel movement boasts a 40-hour power reserve and beats at a frequency of 21,600 vibrations per hour (vph). The F6922 is a reliable and accurate workhorse movement that has been widely praised for its performance and durability.


The Kamasu RA-AA0003R19B is water-resistant to 200 meters (660 feet), making it suitable for recreational diving and other water-based activities. The screw-down crown and case back ensure a secure seal, while the unidirectional bezel allows for easy tracking of elapsed time during dives.
