About us :

Hello, I'm a watch enthusiast who's been fascinated by timepieces for the past couple of years. My goal with this blog is to share my passion for watches with like-minded individuals and provide informative and engaging content about all things horology. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just getting started in the world of watches, I hope you find something of interest here.

I initially started this website as writing and programming practice and only meant it to be for personal use but publishing weekly articles quickly became a hobby of mine. I make reviews, watch guides and watch tierlists for all types of watches from quartz to mechanical, from swiss to japanese and from dress watches to divers so I hope you'll be able to find the next watch for your collection.

Thank you for visiting.
