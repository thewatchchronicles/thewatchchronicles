Top 15 Watches Under $500 : Affordable Timepieces That Look Expensive

Introduction

Watches are not just time-telling devices anymore; they have become an essential accessory that completes your outfit. However, not everyone can afford to spend thousands of dollars on a luxury watch. That's why we have curated a list of the top 15 watches under $500. These timepieces not only look expensive but also offer excellent value for money. In this article, we will take a closer look at these watches and why they deserve a spot on your wrist.

Before we dive into our list, let's go over some basic stats you should keep in mind when shopping for a watch. The diameter refers to the size of the watch face, usually measured in millimeters (mm). Lug-to-lug measures the distance between the lugs, or the parts of the watch that attach to the strap. Strap width and thickness are also important to consider when selecting a watch.
